## Temporary Tattoo Use and Safety

Last updated: June 4, 2026

painter.ink temporary tattoos are designed for external use only.

### How to Use

Apply the tattoo to clean, dry skin. Avoid applying over cuts, irritated skin, sunburn, or sensitive areas. Follow the instructions included with your product page or package.

### Skin Sensitivity

Everyone's skin is different. If you have sensitive skin, allergies, or a history of skin reactions, test a small area first. Stop using the product if redness, itching, discomfort, or irritation occurs.

### Not for Children Without Supervision

Temporary tattoos should be used by children only with adult supervision. Keep packaging and small pieces away from young children.

### External Use Only

Do not apply near eyes, lips, mucous membranes, or broken skin. Do not ingest.
